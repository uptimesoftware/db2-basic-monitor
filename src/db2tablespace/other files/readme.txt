DB2 Tablespace Plug-In Monitor

This plug-in monitor collects statistics for all of the tablespaces defined on the DB2 instance, including tablespace usage and tablespace page utilization metrics.

The following statistics are collected by the monitor: 
- Tablespace Used (KB)
- Tablespace Available (KB)
- Tablespace Used (%)
- Used Pages in all Tablespaces
- Free Pages in all Tablespaces
